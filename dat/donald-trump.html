Top Priorities

  • Creating jobs: Stimulate the economy through job creation. He has called himself “the greatest jobs president God ever created.”
  • Fighting the Islamic State: He said he would, “bomb the hell out of” Iraqi oil fields in order to weaken ISIS.
  • Strengthening foreign policy: Thinks we need to negotiate more effectively with foreign powers.

Donald’s Resumé

  • 1964-1968
    Attended Fordham University and transferred to the Wharton School of Business.
  • 1968-1971
    Worked at his father's company, Elizabeth Trump and Son.
  • 1971-present
    Chairman and CEO of The Trump Organization.
  • 2004-present
    Host of The Celebrity Apprentice.

Highs

  • 1990: Acquired the Taj Mahal in Atlantic City, New Jersey, which was the largest casino complex “ever built”.
  • 1987: His first book, Trump: The Art of the Deal, became a New York Times best seller.
  • 1984: Construction of the Trump Tower in New York City was completed.

Lows
