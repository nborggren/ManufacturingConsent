Pete Rose gives his full support to Donald Trump as only he can

By Nunzio Ingrassia

Published March 13, 2016
Facebook0 Twitter0 Email Print

CINCINNATI, OH - JANUARY 19: Former Cincinnati Reds player Pete Rose, attends a press conference at the Champions Club at Great American Ball Park on January 19, 2016 in Cincinnati, Ohio. Rose was introduced as the latest member of the Cincinnati Reds Hall of Fame and will be inducted at a game in June. (Photo by Mark Lyons/Getty Images)

Donald Trump is getting some backing from the world of sports and now you can add the Hit King to that list. Pete Rose gave his endorsement to Trump for President, sending him a signed baseball with a message to The Donald.

Just received from @PeteRose_14.
Thank you Pete! #VoteTrump on
Tuesday Ohio! #Trump2016#MakeAmericaGreatAgain pic.twitter.com/UvP03n7dQq
